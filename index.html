Four-way Traffic Light System

Guided by Maitrayee Handique NPS International School
--Submitted by Pipal R. Deka Mahir D. Chaudhry

The components used in this project are -

  • LCD screen
  • Breadboard.
  • Arduino UNO.
  • LED Red and LED Green.
  • Wires.

About the project:

Here, we took 8 LEDs, 4 of Green and 4 of Red colour. We connected them to the Arduino board as OUTPUT pin. Here, after every 30 seconds, traffic lights in alternate roads glow up as shown in the diagram. In the next thirty seconds, the pair of light which is turned off will lit up and the roads will be opened.

We have also added a LCD display in the circuit which will display the time left for the singal to change. We programmed the board according to that to display numbers till thirty seconds and then again start from 0.
